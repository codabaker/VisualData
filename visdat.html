About Cassondra Baker


Experience


Boise State University (2012-2015)

Cassondra's career as a Software Developer began in 2012 when she enrolled in a BS Computer Science degree at her State College. She found that programming "just made sense" to her and she quickly progressed through 3 years of her degree. Her average semester consisted of five to six classes (18 credits) and she wouldn't accept anything lower than a B or 3.5 gpa.

Database Analyst (2016-2017)

In her Junior year of college she was offered a remote position for a local company called Ivinci Health. The first couple of months were very stressful for her, but she had a "never give up" attitude. Her time at Ivinci health taught her many things, including:

  • Managing/creating databases
  • Monitoring and upgrading websites and software
  • MYSQL queries
  • Command Prompt, GIT, and SVN Software

Systems Analyst (2017-Current)

She was then offered a full-time salaried position for a company called MDU. At MDU, she accomplished many things, including:

  • Working on large websites with over 1 million customers
  • Team lead of a property management website
  • Database administrator and Data miner
  • Troubleshooting code problems
  • Writing Technical Documents

What She's Done

Cassondra has an extremely diverse background. Throughout college she was actively involved in the Computer Science Club and Society for Women Engineers. She has a passion for staying caught up on trends and has studied over 15 programming languages as well as made multiple programs and video games in her free time.

Her employment often required working under multiple supervisors, which made her comfortable with multitasking and multiple programming fields.

She was awarded the Presidential Award for Academic Success two different years and has gotten 16 different coursera certificates in programming.
